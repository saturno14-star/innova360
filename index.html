Innovation Diagnostic Framework

INNOVA360 AI helps organizations evaluate their innovation maturity across key pillars using AI insights.

1. Strategic Vision

Evaluates how innovation aligns with the organization's strategic goals and long-term vision.

2. Culture of Innovation

Measures the openness to creativity, experimentation, and learning within teams.

3. Technological Readiness

Assesses how effectively technology is used to enable innovation processes.

4. Leadership & Governance

Examines how leaders promote innovation and support risk-taking in their teams.

5. Collaboration Networks

Analyzes the organization’s ability to collaborate across departments and with external partners.

6. Learning & Adaptability

Measures how the organization learns from results, adapts to change, and evolves over time.

7. Customer-Centric Innovation

Evaluates how customer needs and insights drive the innovation process.
